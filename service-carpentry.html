About This Service

Great carpentry is the backbone of any renovation. Our carpenters handle everything from structural framing and load-bearing wall modifications to custom built-ins, crown moulding, trim work, and deck construction. Every cut is precise, every joint is tight. We build custom kitchen and bathroom cabinetry, closet systems, entertainment units, mudroom lockers, and outdoor decks and pergolas. Whether it's rough framing or fine finishing carpentry, Slate & Oak delivers quality that's built to last.

  • ✓ Custom cabinetry & built-ins
  • ✓ Structural framing & load walls
  • ✓ Deck & pergola construction
  • ✓ Crown moulding & trim work
  • ✓ Closet & storage systems
  • ✓ Stairs & railing installation
