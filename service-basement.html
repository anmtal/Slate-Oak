Basement Finishing

Turn unused space into your favourite room.

About This Service

A finished basement can add significant value to your home and provide the extra living space your family needs. Slate & Oak manages the entire process — from framing and insulation through to drywall, flooring, electrical, and plumbing — all under one roof. We build legal basement suites (with separate entrances and rental income potential), home theatres, gyms, home offices, and playrooms. All basement work is fully permitted through the City and built to Ontario Building Code standards.

  • ✓ Legal basement suites
  • ✓ Home theatre & entertainment rooms
  • ✓ Home gym & office spaces
  • ✓ Full framing, insulation & drywall
  • ✓ Egress windows & waterproofing
  • ✓ City permit management
