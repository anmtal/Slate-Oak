About This Service

Our licensed electricians handle everything from simple fixture swaps to full electrical panel upgrades and whole-home rewiring. All work is completed to Ontario Electrical Safety Code standards, fully permitted, and ESA inspected where required. We specialize in pot light installations, smart home wiring, EV charger rough-ins, and basement electrical for new finished spaces. Whether you're renovating or simply need an upgrade, our electrical team delivers clean, safe, and reliable work every time.

  • ✓ Panel upgrades (100A–200A)
  • ✓ Pot light & fixture installation
  • ✓ EV charger rough-in & install
  • ✓ Full home rewiring
  • ✓ Basement electrical rough-in
  • ✓ ESA permit & inspection
