About This Service

The right flooring transforms a room. Our flooring specialists install hardwood, engineered wood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), ceramic tile, porcelain, and natural stone across all areas of your home — from main floor living spaces to bathrooms and basements. We handle subfloor preparation, levelling, waterproofing membranes, and heated floor systems. Every installation is done with precision — no shortcuts, no squeaks, no lippage. We supply materials or work with what you've selected.

  • ✓ Hardwood & engineered wood
  • ✓ Luxury vinyl plank (LVP)
  • ✓ Ceramic & porcelain tile
  • ✓ Natural stone installation
  • ✓ Heated floor systems
  • ✓ Subfloor prep & levelling
